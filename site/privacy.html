Privacy policy

Your basket is personal.

RandBasket is designed to collect as little personal information as possible while giving you a useful grocery comparison.

Last updated15 July 2026
01

Your basket stays local

Your saved basket and preferences remain in your browser unless you choose to send information to us.

02

You choose what to share

Name and email are optional when you send a suggestion. The message itself is required so we can understand it.

03

We do not sell your details

Personal information submitted to RandBasket is used to operate, protect and improve the service.

01

What we collect

Basket and comparison information

Search phrases, selected products, quantities, retailer choices and comparison settings are used to build your results. Saved basket information is stored in your browser.

Optional shopping location

If you grant permission, RandBasket uses your approximate coordinates only while the app is open and sends them with a product search or basket comparison to select applicable nearby retailer offers. RandBasket does not save those coordinates in your device state or its catalogue database, create a location history, or track your movement in the background. The app still works with national fallback prices when location is not shared.

Suggestions and support

If you contact us, we receive the information you submit: your message, its category, the page it came from and any optional name or email address you provide.

Technical information

Our hosting and security providers may process ordinary request information such as IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps and security logs to deliver and protect the website.

02

How we use information

  • Provide grocery searches, basket comparisons and retailer product links.
  • Reply to support requests and investigate price or product corrections.
  • Improve reliability, usability, security and retailer matching.
  • Meet legal obligations and prevent misuse of the service.

03

Service providers and retailer links

RandBasket uses service providers for website hosting, security, databases and email delivery. They may process information only as needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information when required by law or necessary to protect users and the service.

Retailer links take you to third-party websites. Their privacy practices and purchases are governed by their own policies, not this policy.

04

Your choices and rights

You can clear saved basket data through your browser. You may also ask us to access, correct or delete personal information you submitted, or object to certain processing. These requests are handled subject to applicable South African law.

We do not use suggestion contact details for marketing unless you clearly ask or consent to receive it.

05

Retention and security

We keep submitted information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond, improve RandBasket, maintain security and meet legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards, but no online service can promise absolute security.

Privacy contact

Questions or requests?

Email us and tell us what you need. We may ask for enough information to confirm the request relates to you.
