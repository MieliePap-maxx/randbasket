Pre-launch policy

Privacy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

RandBasket is preparing for public release. This page will be finalised before the mobile app is publicly available.

What the app is designed to use

The app uses grocery searches, basket entries, retailer product links and price-comparison data to provide the service. We will minimise personal data collection and will not require an account for the initial release unless an account is needed for a clear user benefit.

Price data

Retailer product information and prices can change by location, availability, fulfilment method and promotions. RandBasket will show a freshness timestamp where available.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@randbasket.co.za.
