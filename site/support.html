Quick answers

Before you send a message

These are the most common things people need to know about RandBasket.

Why is a retailer price different?

Prices may vary by location, account, fulfilment method, stock and promotion eligibility. RandBasket shows the product information available to it; always confirm at checkout.

How is the best price chosen?

RandBasket compares equivalent units—such as price per kilogram or litre—when product size information is available. Pack contents and product types can still differ, so check the selected item.

Where is my basket saved?

Your basket and settings are saved in your browser on that device. Clearing site data or using another device may give you a separate basket.

Does RandBasket sell groceries?

No. RandBasket is an independent comparison tool. Purchases and deliveries happen through the retailer you choose.

Can I request access to or deletion of my information?

Yes. Email us using the address below and describe your request. See the Privacy page for more detail.
