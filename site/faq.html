Prices and retailers

What the numbers can tell you

Which supermarket is the cheapest in South Africa?

There is no single retailer that is cheapest for every shopper or every basket. The result depends on the products, pack sizes, brands, location, stock and promotions available at the time. RandBasket is designed to compare the basket you actually want rather than make a permanent claim about one retailer.

Are grocery prices the same everywhere?

No. Prices and availability can vary by store, region, account, loyalty membership, fulfilment method and promotion. If location-aware information is available, RandBasket can use it to improve relevance; otherwise it may show national or generally published information.

How often are RandBasket prices updated?

Update timing varies by retailer and catalogue source. RandBasket uses the published information available to it, but online catalogues can change without notice. Always confirm the final product and price with the retailer before purchasing.

Which retailers can I compare?

RandBasket currently represents Pick n Pay, Checkers, Woolworths, SPAR and Makro. Coverage and local price availability can differ between them.

Why can a product or price be missing?

Catalogue coverage, location, stock and source availability differ between retailers. A missing result does not necessarily mean the retailer does not sell the product.

Pack-size comparisons

Comparing like with like

How does RandBasket compare different pack sizes?

Compatible measurements are converted into a shared unit. For example, 250 g, 500 g and 1 kg products can be compared using price per kilogram. Millilitres and litres can be compared using price per litre.

What does the green best-price label mean?

It identifies the lowest comparable unit price among the relevant results currently shown. It does not guarantee that the products have identical brands, ingredients, quality, dietary characteristics or promotion conditions.

Is the cheapest pack always the best value?

No. The pack with the lowest checkout total may contain less product. A larger pack can cost more overall while costing less per kilogram, litre or item. Your budget, storage space and likely usage still matter.

Can different brands be compared?

Yes, when they represent a similar product and compatible measurement, but the comparison is about price and quantity. Brand preference, ingredients and quality remain personal choices.

What happens when a pack size is missing?

The product may still appear as a relevant match, but RandBasket cannot make a dependable unit-price comparison without a trustworthy quantity and measurement.

Using RandBasket

Your basket and your choices

Does RandBasket sell or deliver groceries?

No. RandBasket is an independent comparison service. Purchases, payments, collections, deliveries and returns are completed through the retailer you choose.

Where is my grocery basket saved?

Your basket and preferences are normally saved in your browser on your device. Clearing browser data or switching browsers or devices may give you a separate basket.

Does sharing my location track me?

No location history is created by RandBasket. If you grant permission, approximate coordinates are used with the current comparison to select relevant nearby offers and are not saved as part of your basket.

Why does RandBasket open a retailer website?

The retailer link lets you review the source product and continue shopping with that retailer. The retailer’s website controls the final price, stock, account requirements and checkout.

How can I report an incorrect price or product?

Use the Suggestions option in the app or visit Support. Include the retailer, product name, price and approximate time. A screenshot or retailer link can help us investigate.
