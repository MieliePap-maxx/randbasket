Local prices

May RandBasket use your location?

Why we ask: SPAR and other grocers can show different prices and availability at different branches. Your approximate location helps RandBasket choose offers from stores near you.

Your privacy: We use your location only for searches and price checks while RandBasket is open. We do not save your coordinates, create a location history, or track you in the background.
