### Description

RandBasket helps South African shoppers compare grocery products, pack sizes and listed prices with less guesswork.

Search for the groceries you need, compare available retailer matches and add the products that suit your basket. RandBasket makes unit pricing clearer, so a larger shelf price does not hide better value per kilogram, litre or item.

Use RandBasket to:

- Search grocery products across supported retailers.
- Browse verified catalogue specials and add them to your basket.
- Compare products and pack sizes fairly.
- Build and save a basket on your device.
- Compare available basket totals.
- Use optional approximate location for more relevant nearby pricing.
- Follow retailer links to confirm products, prices and availability.

Prices, promotions, stock and availability can vary by location, branch, account, fulfilment method and retailer terms. Always confirm the final product and price with the retailer before buying.

RandBasket is an independent comparison service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the retailers shown unless explicitly stated.

### Full description

Compare groceries with less guesswork.

RandBasket helps South African shoppers search grocery products, compare available retailer matches and build a clearer weekly basket. Compare pack sizes using unit prices per kilogram, litre or item, then save your basket on your device.

FEATURES

• Search products across supported South African grocery retailers  
• Browse verified catalogue specials and add offers to your basket  
• Compare listed prices and pack sizes fairly  
• Build and save a grocery basket on your phone  
• Compare available basket totals  
• Use optional approximate location for more relevant nearby prices  
• Open retailer product pages to confirm the final details

IMPORTANT PRICE INFORMATION

Prices, promotions, stock and availability can vary by location, branch, account, fulfilment method and retailer terms. RandBasket provides comparison information, not a guaranteed checkout price. Always confirm the product, pack size, promotion and final price with the retailer before buying.

PRIVACY

Your basket and Smart Basket shortcuts are stored on your device. Location is optional, used only while the app is open, and is not used for background tracking or location history. Optional anonymous basket activity is off by default and can be deleted by switching it off.

RandBasket is an independent comparison service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the retailers shown unless explicitly stated.
